M1: Faces for Visual Communication

Photoshop, Word, PowerPoint
Oct 13th, 2021

For the course Visual Communication we had to design faces. First we needed to make a stroy from some headlines from the New York Times. Based on this story, you needed to design 3 faces that matched the story. Every face also has a tonality word, like Relief, Love or Stress. This was my story:

Meet two lovers,
Ted and Katrina’s future will never be same
after Ted becomes a famous scientist.
However, he’s slowly losing his mind by all the pressure.
His love Katrina saved him from ruin by making retirement plans.
They will ditch the city and run a farm.
It was just what they needed.

These were my first 3 faces:

The next assignment was to make a 3d-face, this could be made out of anything. I decided that i wanted to make a 3d-heart, since that would fit with my story. After some painting and glueing, this was my 3d face:
The next assignment was to re-brand your faces. You now didn't need the story anymore. You needed to come up with a popular brand that would fit one of your faces. We then needed to present our faces with the logo of this famous brand, and with a selfmade slogan that would fit the style of the company.
