M5: Oly

Unity, FMOD, Blender, Presenting, Teamwork, Writing
Jan 18th, 2023

"Oly" is my biggest and most ambitious project so far on this website. Together with Natan for the core project of Module 5B, we created a "sophisticated" game about a little robot. Oly finds his way to an abandoned research facility in the middle of the dessert. The player can freely explore the facility and unlock new abilities to progress deeper into the building. Can you help Oly finish Project: Olympus?

The game turned out to be an amazing experience according to all teachers, we scored a 10/10 for all aspects: Game Technology, Game Design and Sound Engineering!
