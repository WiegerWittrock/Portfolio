M2: Microsoft 365 Collaborating

Microsoft Collaboration
Jan 6th, 2022

I followed an online training from Goodhabitz.
The training was about the collaboration between microsoft office 365 apps. I chose this training because we use the Microsoft applications very often for school, but also for personal use. Improving my knowledge about these apps and how you can collaborate with them, will help me in my further development as a professional.
The training consist of 5 lessons, each starting with a manual followed by examples. All the examples have questions you have to answer yourself to make sure you understand and remember. A lesson is finished if you complete all the examples. The entire training is finished when you pass the final test.
