M4: Algorithms

Processing, Algorithms
Oct 24th, 2022

For the Algorithms course in module 4 we learned multiple interesting algorithms used in programming. For example, we learned flocking, forces, mass-spring-damper-systems, perlin noise and particle effects in the Processing. The final assignment for the course was to make a big project that combines these algoriths. We made a game where you could shoot a ball with a catapult (forces) to UFO's (flocking) that would spat (particles) if you hit them. We also added a mass-spring-damper-system for the grass so it moves with the wind and we randomly generated mountains in the background with perlin noise. This is what we ended up with:
