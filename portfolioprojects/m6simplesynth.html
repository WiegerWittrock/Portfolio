M6: Simple Synth

Unity, Arduino, Teamwork, Presenting, Sound design
Apr 2nd, 2023

For the core project of Module 6, I made a modular synthesiser in a group of five people. The product is called Symple Synth and it is a simplified version of a digital synthesiser. The goal of Simple Synth was to make digital synthesisers easier to understand for young adults. From our research we found that the product worked!
Users can connect all sorts of wooden blocks to eachother. Different combinations make different sounds. We also made a digital environment in Unity so it is easier to see what is going on.
The project was graded with a 9.2 out of 10!
