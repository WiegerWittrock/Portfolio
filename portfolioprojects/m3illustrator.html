I followed an online Illustrator course on YouTube.
The course consists of 19 small lessons about Adobe Illustrator. Illustrator is a vector-based designing and drawing program. It looks a bit like Photoshop, but it used for drawings and designing. Now it became more serious. He explained new tools that made it possible to draw anything, so not only squares and circles. I practiced this by copying shapes. Also more coloring methods were introduced.

Illustrator is often used to edit pictures, for example by adding shapes or converting the picture into a drawing and editing the colors.

You can also edit text in many different ways.

I really liked the course. I learnt a lot of things and Illustrator is very usefull program to increase the aestetics of your reports or presentations for example.
