M5: PS5 Cover

Photoshop
Oct 30th, 2022

For the challenge of Module 5 I decided to make my own PS5 cover. I chose to re-create the cover for the popular PlayStation game FIFA 23. Currently, two players are on the cover because they have a contract with the game company. The football fans however are fed up with the same players on the cover for the third game in a row. Me being one of them, I decided to put Cristiano Ronaldo and Lionel Messi on the cover, as this year they will both play their final World Cup. First I followed online photoshop courses, after that I started from scratch on the project.
