M1: Programming

Processing, Programming, Teamwork
Oct 17th, 2021

This course consists of multiple programming exercises. The first exercise was to make a creature in the programming langauge Processing. I was busy making a bird, and it is now finished! In the following pictures you can see the design tree and you can see the birds in its full glory!

As you can see, a lot has changed since I posted the blog post. Now there are many birds, and they actually flap their wings and go up and down. You can also drag a bird and throw it to a wall, it will even bounce back (no wonder the birds are fleeing from you...). You can reset the position and the speed of the birds, and finally, you can change their colors!
