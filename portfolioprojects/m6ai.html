M6: Simple Synth

Python, Pygame, AI
Apr 2nd, 2023

During Module 6 I learned a lot about AI programs. For this course we learned theory and we put the theory into practice. First we had to make smaller python programms that focused on various types of algorithms that AI uses like trees, recursion, inheritance and heuristics. For the final assignment of the course I had to make an AI program that uses some of these algorithms. Together with Natan I made a game about shooting cowboys using handtracking. In this game, cowboys find the shortest path through a random maze using AI, but you can shoot them using handtracking.

"In Yee-Haw your train has crashed and a bunch of cowboys walking through a maze are out to get your gold, you can defend your gold by shooting the cowboys with a cannon, but if five cowboys reach your gold, you lose! The cowboys spawn faster and faster until it becomes very hard to defend!"

The project was graded 10 out of 10!
